Major Achievements

  • Daesang Awards (Grand Prizes): EXO has been honored with multiple Daesang awards at prestigious South Korean music award ceremonies, including the Golden Disk Awards, Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA), and Seoul Music Awards. These awards recognize their exceptional contributions to the K-Pop industry.
  • Million-Selling Albums: EXO's albums, such as "XOXO," "EXODUS," and "EX'ACT," have achieved extraordinary sales figures, surpassing the one-million mark. Their albums have consistently topped music charts and set records in the industry.
  • Chart-Topping Hits: EXO's songs, including "Growl," "Call Me Baby," and "Love Shot," have dominated music charts both in South Korea and internationally. They frequently achieve "all-kill" status, indicating their popularity and widespread success.
  • World Tours: EXO's world tours have taken them to countries across Asia, North America, Europe, and beyond. They have performed in iconic venues and arenas, captivating fans with their mesmerizing performances and elaborate stage productions.
  • Global Fanbase - EXO-L: EXO-L is one of the most dedicated and passionate fanbases in the K-Pop world. With millions of members worldwide, EXO-Ls actively support the group through fan clubs, fan events, and online communities.
  • MTV Europe Music Awards: EXO received international recognition by winning the "Best Korean Act" award at the MTV Europe Music Awards. This achievement highlighted their global impact and popularity.
  • Social Media Dominance: EXO's social media presence is formidable, with millions of followers on platforms like Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube. Their posts and content regularly go viral, showcasing their influence on social media trends.
  • Korean Wave (Hallyu): EXO has played a pivotal role in the global spread of K-Pop and Korean culture. Their music videos, such as "Monster" and "Tempo," have garnered hundreds of millions of views on YouTube, contributing to the Hallyu phenomenon.
  • Endorsements: EXO members, both as a group and individually, have been sought after by major brands for endorsements. They have become influential figures in the fields of fashion, beauty, and consumer products.
  • Charitable Initiatives: Some EXO members, such as Suho, have been actively involved in charitable activities. They use their fame and resources to support various social causes and charitable organizations, making a positive impact on society.
  • Cultural Impact: EXO's influence extends beyond music. Their distinctive fashion, choreography, and music style have left an enduring impact on K-Pop and pop culture. They are often credited with shaping the direction of the industry and inspiring future generations of artists.
